Right time



It is believed that autumn has no inappropriate time for shooting. And this is seriously so. Morning or early afternoon can reward the photographer with interesting shadows in the picture. An early October morning can give a real magical and mysterious fog, or frost, which will provide a soft background and long shadows (by the way, they can also be used gracefully when shooting). And the golden sunlight just before sunset is able to completely transform the autumn weather that seemed different earlier.

Frame layout



The most important thing is not to leave a void in the picture. The autumn photo shoot should emphasize the details of the withering of nature, as well as the beauty of its charm during this period. We must try to fill in the frame. If the sky is necessarily clouds or clouds. If the foreground is any vegetation, be it a shrub, a flower bed or anything else. And so on. If you neglect this moment, the photos will look lifeless and made hastily. If it is not possible to choose a specific environment, then cropping can help.
